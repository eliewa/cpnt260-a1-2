ABOUT US

Mission Statement: "Our goal as a combat sports retailer is to provide the best quality gear for our dedicated athletes. We believe that the same effort that goes into fighting in the ring should also be put into the development of the most cutting edge combat equipment. That is why you can trust that whatever we sell has been tried, tested and approved by the best in the industry." - Eli Ewa, Cody Garbrandt

History: Born out of the struggle of mixed martial arts and constantly seeking to get to the next level we started this company in 2015 in a humble garage just selling custom boxing gloves to friends and family. Now flash forward seven years and we have been blessed to become the number one mixed martial arts retailer in Canada.
